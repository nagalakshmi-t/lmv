About Our Company

Our vision

We started LMV Group on 4th SEPT 2015 with LMVFS with a VISION to build India's best Financial Services Company and to reach every corner of India. We have been successful in building our company and in the same by extending our operations to HYDERABAD, CHENNAI, MUMBAI, PUNE, VIZAG, BANGLORE, AND VIJAYAWADA.

We also started our next group of companies by entering into the Insurance sector with LMVIB in Feb 2018 to fill the gap in the industry by providing better insurance services to clients. As technology is the major journey to change the model of business we have started our own Technology company LMVIT Solutions Pvt Ltd in 2019 May which is now helping us in building the technology for all our group companies with our own inhouse projects like CRM, Payroll, Business applications and many more under developing stages.

We have even gone further and expanded our group of companies into Wealth Management by forming LMV INVESTMENT SERVICES in JAN 2020. Even we expanded our branch offices in different parts of Hyderabad’s - Miyapur, KUKATPALLY, & Nagole. We as a group have been growing at a very good pace and path. We are looking forward to meeting our vision of building India’s best financial company and helping our Indians to make money by providing the best financial advice to them.
